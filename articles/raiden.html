⚡ 角色定位与核心机制Role & Core Mechanics

雷电将军 — 五星雷元素长柄武器角色，定位为充能辅助+爆发型副C/主C。她的核心机制围绕「诸愿百眼之轮」展开——队友释放元素爆发可为雷神叠加愿力层数，层数越高，雷神开启「梦想一心」状态后的伤害倍率越高。

Raiden Shogun — 5★ Electro Polearm character. Role: Energy battery + Burst DPS/Sub-DPS. Her core mechanic revolves around the Chakra Desiderata: allies casting Elemental Bursts stack Resolve stacks, and the more stacks, the higher Raiden's damage multiplier during her Musou Isshin state.

一句话总结：雷电将军同时解决了队伍的充能循环和爆发输出两个问题。她能给全队充能、能给全队增伤（元素爆发伤害），自己还能打出 20~50 万+ 的大招核爆。

TL;DR: Raiden solves both energy generation and burst damage problems simultaneously. She recharges the entire team, buffs their Burst DMG, and can deal 200K-500K+ burst nukes herself.

🔑 核心天赋优先级

🔑 Talent Priority

元素爆发 (Q) > 元素战技 (E) > 普通攻击 (A)Elemental Burst (Q) > Elemental Skill (E) > Normal Attack (A)

大招是雷神的一切。E技能提供全队元素爆发增伤（基于能量消耗），持续时间25秒，覆盖率高。普攻在非梦想一心状态下基本不用点。

Her Burst is everything. Elemental Skill provides team-wide Burst DMG bonus (based on energy cost) with 25s duration — essentially permanent uptime. Normal Attack talents can be ignored outside Musou Isshin.

🗡️ 武器选择 · 5档排名Weapon Ranking (5 Tiers)

雷神对武器的核心需求：充能 > 暴击 > 攻击力。充能武器能同时提升循环流畅度和雷伤加成（绝缘4+固有天赋双重转化），收益远超纯攻击武器。

Raiden's weapon priority: ER > CRIT > ATK. ER weapons simultaneously improve rotation consistency and Electro DMG bonus (via Emblem 4pc + passive conversion), far outweighing pure ATK weapons.

❓ 常见问题 FAQFAQ

充能沙还是攻击沙？

ER Sands or ATK Sands?

首选充能沙。非专武情况下充能沙收益远高于攻击沙。只有带薙草且副词条充能已到250%+才考虑换攻击沙。

ER Sands first. Without Engulfing Lightning, ER sands greatly outperforms ATK. Only consider ATK sands with Engulfing + 250%+ ER from substats.

0命能玩吗？

Is C0 viable?

完全可以。0命雷神在雷国队中已经是核心引擎。C2是加分项不是必选项，先抽角色0命配渔获就能打通深渊。

Absolutely. C0 Raiden is already the core engine of Rational team. C2 is a bonus, not a requirement. C0 + The Catch can full-star Abyss.

雷伤杯还是攻击杯？

Electro DMG or ATK Goblet?

攻击杯。因为雷神自身（固有天赋+绝缘4）已经给了大量增伤区，攻击杯在大多数配队中略优于雷伤杯（差距约3-5%）。哪个副词条好用哪个。

ATK Goblet. Raiden already has massive DMG% from her passive + Emblem 4pc. ATK Goblet slightly edges out Electro DMG in most teams (~3-5% gap). Use whichever has better substats.
