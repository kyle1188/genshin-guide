🌊 角色定位 · 重击水龙炮机制Role & Charged Attack Mechanics

那维莱特 — 五星水元素法器角色，定位为主C输出（Hydro Hypercarry）。核心机制是重击「衡平推裁」（水龙炮）造成大范围水元素伤害并自我治疗。A1被动根据队友触发的水元素反应数量，提供最高160%的重击伤害加成。源水灵珠（来自E/Q）可大幅缩短重击蓄力时间。

Neuvillette — 5★ Hydro Catalyst character. Role: Main DPS (Hydro Hypercarry). His core mechanic is the charged attack "Equitable Judgment" (water cannon) dealing massive AoE Hydro DMG while self-healing. His A1 passive grants up to 160% CA DMG bonus based on party members triggering Hydro reactions. Sourcewater droplets (from E/Q) speed up charged attack charge time.

一句话总结：那维莱特是游戏中最强的AoE主C——重击水龙炮自带穿透+自疗，0命即可单通深渊12层。搭配芙宁娜可实现T0级别的枫丹水龙队。

TL;DR: Neuvillette is the strongest AoE Main DPS in the game — his charged attack "water cannon" has innate penetration + self-healing, capable of solo-clearing Abyss Floor 12 at C0. Pair with Furina for the T0 Fontaine Hydro Dragon team.

🔑 核心天赋优先级

🔑 Talent Priority

普通攻击 (重击) > 元素战技 (E) ≥ 元素爆发 (Q)Normal Attack (CA) > Elemental Skill (E) ≥ Elemental Burst (Q)

关键：普攻天赋等级直接决定重击伤害，必须优先满级。A1被动：队友每触发1种水相关反应，获得1层「昔日龙荣」（最多3层 = 110%/125%/160%重击伤害加成）。

Key: NA talent levels are critical as CA damage scales directly with NA level. A1 passive: For each unique Hydro-related reaction triggered by party members, gain 1 stack of "Past Draconic Glories" (max 3 stacks = 110%/125%/160% CA DMG at 1/2/3 stacks).

📊 源水灵珠机制

📊 Sourcewater Droplet Mechanics

E技能和Q爆发会生成源水灵珠。拾取灵珠可：①立即减少重击蓄力时间；②恢复生命值；③提供1层A1被动（若灵珠来自其他水角色触发的反应）。最多持有6颗灵珠。

Elemental Skill and Burst generate Sourcewater Droplets. Picking up droplets: ① immediately reduces charged attack charge time; ② restores HP; ③ provides 1 A1 passive stack (if droplet came from reactions triggered by other Hydro characters). Max 6 droplets can be held.

Neuvillette's weapon priority: CRIT DMG/CRIT Rate > HP% > Elemental Mastery. Charged Attack DMG is the core; HP influences healing and A1 passive trigger frequency.

❓ 常见问题 FAQFAQ

HP杯还是水伤杯？

HP Goblet or Hydro DMG Goblet?

水伤杯约优3%，但HP%杯副属性更好时选HP%杯。48K HP是HP杯的阈值，达标后水伤杯更优。

Hydro DMG Goblet is ~3% better, but HP% Goblet with better substats wins. 48K HP is the HP Goblet threshold — once reached, Hydro DMG Goblet is superior.

充能需求多少？

How much ER do I need?

120%单水是最低要求。双水队可降至110%。试作金珀用户需求更低（武器被动回能）。不足会导致Q循环断档。

120% solo Hydro is the minimum. Double Hydro can drop to 110%. Prototype Amber users need even less (weapon passive regens energy). Insufficient ER causes Q rotation gaps.

0命值得抽吗？

Is C0 worth pulling?

绝对值得。0命那维莱特是游戏中最强的AoE主C，能够单通深渊12层。C1提供关键的抗打断，C2是伤害分水岭。

Absolutely yes. C0 Neuvillette is the strongest AoE DPS in the game, capable of solo-clearing Abyss Floor 12. C1 provides essential interruption resistance, C2 is the DMG powerspike.
