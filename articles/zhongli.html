🪨 角色定位 · 护盾与减抗机制详解Role & Shield Mechanics

钟离 — 五星岩元素长柄武器角色，定位为护盾辅助 / 爆发副C / 全元素减抗（岩王帝君）。元素战技长按生成游戏最强护盾，对所有元素伤害和物理伤害拥有150%吸收效率，同时为周围敌人施加20%全元素减抗光环（含物理）。元素爆发「天星」造成大范围岩元素伤害，并石化敌人3-6秒。

Zhongli — 5★ Geo Polearm character. Role: Shield Support / Burst Sub-DPS / Universal RES Shredder (Geo Archon). His Elemental Skill (hold) creates the strongest shield in the game with 150% absorption efficiency vs all damage types, while applying a 20% universal RES shred aura (all elements + physical). His Burst "Planet Befall" deals massive AoE Geo DMG and petrifies enemies for 3–6 seconds.

一句话总结：钟离是原神最强的生存辅助——只要长按E，你就能获得一个持续20秒、CD仅12秒的超厚护盾，同时降低周围敌人全抗性。黑缨枪+全生命圣遗物就能毕业，全游性价比最高的角色。

TL;DR: Zhongli is the ultimate comfort pick — hold E for a 20s shield (12s CD) with unmatched durability, paired with a 20% universal RES shred aura. Black Tassel + full HP artifacts is all you need for endgame. The most cost-efficient character in the game.

🔑 核心天赋优先级

🔑 Talent Priority

元素战技 (E) >> 元素爆发 (Q) > 普通攻击 (A)Elemental Skill (E) >> Elemental Burst (Q) > Normal Attack (A)

E是核心护盾来源，优先升满。Q提供石化控制和爆发伤害。普攻可不点（非物理C玩法下完全不用）。

Skill creates the core shield — max it first. Burst provides petrification CC and burst damage. Normal Attack can be skipped (unused outside physical DPS builds).

🛡️ 玉璋护盾机制

🛡️ Jade Shield Mechanics

长按E生成玉璋护盾，吸收量完全基于钟离最大生命值。LV10时基础吸收量 = 23.04% Max HP + 2712。典型5万HP出装下，基础盾量约14,000 HP。由于岩元素护盾对所有伤害类型拥有150%吸收效率，等效吸收量 ≈ 21,000+ HP。持续20秒，CD仅12秒，实现100%覆盖。护盾存在期间，周围敌人全元素抗性与物理抗性降低20%。

Hold E creates Jade Shield — absorption scales purely with Zhongli's Max HP. At LV10, base absorption = 23.04% Max HP + 2712. With a typical 50K HP build, that's ~14,000 HP base shield. Since Geo shields have 150% absorption efficiency vs all damage types, effective absorption ≈ 21,000+ HP. 20s duration with 12s CD = 100% uptime. While the shield is active, enemies' all-element and physical RES is reduced by 20%.

🏛️ 岩脊共鸣

🏛️ Stone Stele Resonance

点按E或长按E同时生成岩脊（Stone Stele）。岩脊会与场上其他岩造物产生共鸣，造成范围岩元素伤害。在纯岩队中，多个岩造物同时共鸣可产生可观的后台伤害。

Tap E or hold E also summons a Stone Stele. The Stele resonates with other Geo constructs on the field, dealing AoE Geo DMG. In mono-Geo teams, multiple constructs resonating simultaneously can generate meaningful off-field damage.

🗡️ 武器选择 · 6档排名Weapon Ranking (6 Tiers)

钟离的武器需求取决于玩法：纯护盾辅助 → 生命%优先；爆发副C → 暴击/暴伤 > 攻击%。纯盾用黑缨枪即可，副C需要五星武器。

Zhongli's weapon priority depends on build: Pure shield support → HP% first; Burst Sub-DPS → CRIT > ATK%. Black Tassel suffices for pure shield; 5★ weapons are needed for burst DPS builds.

💡 For pure shield support? Black Tassel R5! It's a 3★ weapon that provides 46.9% HP — all you need for a 50K+ HP shield. Zero wishes required. Staff of Homa is only needed for burst support Zhongli.

❓ 常见问题 FAQFAQ

护盾流还是爆发流？

Shield or Burst build?

纯护盾流（5万+HP）更安全更通用。爆发流（3万HP + 暴击）牺牲护盾强度换取约10万一发的天星伤害。日常使用推荐护盾流。

Pure shield build (50K+ HP) is safer and more universal. Burst build (30K HP + CRIT) sacrifices shield strength for ~100K meteor hits. Choose shield for general use.

生命杯还是岩伤杯？

HP goblet or Geo goblet?

护盾流选生命杯，爆发流选岩伤杯。永远不要带攻击力杯——钟离的技能伤害和护盾都不吃攻击力杯的收益。

HP% goblet for shield build. Geo DMG goblet for burst build. Never use ATK% — Zhongli's skill damage and shield both gain negligible value from ATK% goblet.

0命值得抽吗？

Is C0 worth pulling?

值得，绝对值得。钟离是全游戏提升游戏体验最大的角色。他的护盾能让95%的游戏内容变得毫无压力。如果你感觉操作吃力或者总是暴毙，抽他就对了。

YES. Absolutely. Zhongli is the single best quality-of-life upgrade in the game. His shield trivializes 95% of content. If you struggle with dodging or survivability, pull him immediately.
