🔥 ① 玛薇卡蒸发队 — T0 版本答案① Mavuika Vaporize — T0 Meta Answer

T0 🌊🔥 蒸发蒸发！Vaporize for Days!
玛薇卡 (主C)Mavuika (Main DPS) 芙宁娜 (挂水+增伤)Furina (Hydro app+DMG buff) 希诺宁 (减抗)Xilonen (RES shred) 班尼特 (增伤+治疗)Bennett (ATK buff+Heal)

输出手法：芙宁娜 E(黑芙) Q → 希诺宁 E Q 减抗 → 班尼特 Q → 玛薇卡 Q(核爆) → 强化重击×4~5

芙宁娜EQ挂水覆盖全程，玛薇卡Q蒸发核爆可打50万+。12-3上半稳定40秒通关。双火共鸣+芙宁娜增伤75%+希诺宁减抗36%+班尼特加攻——四重增伤叠加，输出爆炸。

💡 替代：没有芙宁娜换行秋（伤害低20%），没有希诺宁换万叶。

Rotation: Furina E(Ousia) Q → Xilonen E Q RES shred → Bennett Q → Mavuika Q(Nuke) → Enhanced Charged ATK×4~5

Furina's EQ maintains full Hydro uptime, Mavuika's Q Vaporize nuke can hit 500K+. Clears 12-3 first half in a stable 40 seconds. Double Pyro resonance + Furina's 75% DMG bonus + Xilonen's 36% RES shred + Bennett's ATK buff — quadruple stacking, insane damage.

💡 Substitutes: No Furina → Xingqiu (20% less damage), No Xilonen → Kazuha.

🌊 ② 那维莱特队 — T0 水龙无双② Neuvillette — T0 Hydro Dragon Supreme

T0 🌊 审判之时！Time for Judgment!
那维莱特 (主C)Neuvillette (Main DPS) 芙宁娜 (增伤)Furina (DMG buff) 万叶 (减抗聚怪)Kazuha (RES shred+Gather) 白术 (群奶)Baizhu (Team Heal)

输出手法：芙宁娜 E Q → 万叶 E Q → 白术 E Q 群奶 → 那维莱特 E Q → 蓄力重击×3

操作最简单、容错率最高的T0队。那维莱特自烧自回完美触发芙宁娜叠层，白术瞬奶拉满增伤。一喷伤害3~5万×8段，15秒打完。12-3下半两个Boss都可以一喷到底。

💡 替代：没有白术换夏洛蒂/瑶瑶，没有万叶换砂糖。

Rotation: Furina E Q → Kazuha E Q → Baizhu E Q team heal → Neuvillette E Q → Charged ATK×3

The easiest T0 team with the highest error margin. Neuvillette's self-HP drain and recovery perfectly stacks Furina's buff, Baizhu's instant heal maximizes DMG bonus. Each charged attack deals 30K~50K×8 hits, clearing in 15s. Both Bosses in 12-3 second half can be swept in one rotation.

💡 Substitutes: No Baizhu → Charlotte/Yaoyao, No Kazuha → Sucrose.

⚡ ③ 雷神国家队 — T0.5 经典永存③ Raiden National — T0.5 Timeless Classic

T0.5 ⚡ 雷国！启动！Raiden National! Go!
雷电将军 (充能+站场)Raiden (Energy+On-field) 香菱 (副C)Xiangling (Sub DPS) 行秋 (挂水)Xingqiu (Hydro app) 班尼特 (增伤)Bennett (ATK buff)

输出手法：雷神 E → 班尼特 Q → 行秋 Q E → 香菱 Q E → 雷神 Q → 重击循环×5 → 下一轮

全游戏性价比天花板——1金成本（仅雷神0命），三人四星满命即可。雷神大招给全队充能，让香菱和行秋实现无缝大招循环。版本更迭三年了依然是深渊常客。

💡 雷神无专武用渔获（免费），香菱用渔获/西风，行秋用祭礼剑。

Rotation: Raiden E → Bennett Q → Xingqiu Q E → Xiangling Q E → Raiden Q → Charged ATK loop×5 → Next cycle

The best value-for-cost team in the game — only 1★ cost (Raiden C0), the rest are C6 4-stars. Raiden's Burst restores Energy for the whole team, enabling seamless Burst loops for Xiangling and Xingqiu. Three years of meta shifts and it's still an Abyss staple.

💡 No signature weapon? Raiden uses The Catch (free), Xiangling uses The Catch/Sacrificial, Xingqiu uses Sacrificial Sword.

🌿 ④ 艾尔海森激绽队 — T0.5 须弥之光④ Alhaitham Quicken-Bloom — T0.5 Sumeru's Light

T0.5 🌿 海哥激绽！Alhaitham QB!
艾尔海森 (主C)Alhaitham (Main DPS) 纳西妲 (挂草)Nahida (Dendro app) 行秋 (挂水)Xingqiu (Hydro app) 久岐忍 (种子触发+治疗)Kuki (Hyperbloom+Heal)

输出手法：纳西妲 E Q → 行秋 Q E → 久岐忍 E → 艾尔海森 Q → 普攻×4 → E → 下落攻击 → 普攻×4

激化+超绽放双反应体系。纳西妲提供精通拐，行秋水剑挂水产种子，久岐忍全精通堆到1000+触发3万+超绽放伤害，艾尔海森前台打超激化。完美克制12-2下半雷盾环境。

💡 久岐忍全堆精通（饰金4/乐园4），行秋换夜兰也可以。

Rotation: Nahida E Q → Xingqiu Q E → Kuki E → Alhaitham Q → NA×4 → E → Plunge → NA×4

Dual-reaction Quicken + Hyperbloom system. Nahida provides EM buff, Xingqiu's Hydro swords produce Dendro Cores, Kuki stacks 1000+ full EM to trigger 30K+ Hyperbloom damage, Alhaitham deals Aggravate on-field. Perfect counter to 12-2 second half Electro shields.

💡 Kuki goes full EM (Gilded Dreams/Paradise Lost), Xingqiu can swap to Yelan.

💰 ⑤ 平民低金配队 — 0+0也能满星⑤ F2P Low-Cost Teams — 0+0 Can Also Full-Star

⭐ 0金超绽放队（全四星）⭐ 0★ Hyperbloom Team (All 4-Stars)

草主 + 行秋 + 久岐忍 + 柯莱/瑶瑶

不需要任何五星！草主（免费）挂草、行秋挂水产种、久岐忍触发超绽放。久岐忍90级+乐园4件套+铁蜂刺，一颗种子3万伤害，12-3也能磨过去。

Dendro MC + Xingqiu + Kuki + Collei/Yaoyao

No 5-stars needed! Dendro MC (free) applies Dendro, Xingqiu applies Hydro to produce cores, Kuki triggers Hyperbloom. Kuki at Lv90 + Paradise Lost 4pc + Iron Sting, each core deals 30K damage, can grind through 12-3.

⭐ 1金砂糖国家队⭐ 1★ Sucrose National

砂糖 + 香菱 + 行秋 + 班尼特

没有雷神？砂糖来凑！砂糖站场普攻触发扩散+行秋水剑+香菱火轮，蒸发反应打满。砂糖带讨龙+翠绿4，班尼特带西风。操作比雷国复杂但输出不输。

Sucrose + Xiangling + Xingqiu + Bennett

No Raiden? Sucrose steps in! Sucrose stays on-field with NA triggering Swirl + Xingqiu Hydro swords + Xiangling Pyronado, maxing Vaporize reactions. Sucrose runs Thrilling Tales + VV 4pc, Bennett runs Favonius. Harder to play than Raiden National but comparable damage.

🎯 平民满星核心思路：上半用火/雷主C破12-2冰盾，下半用草/水主C处理雷盾。不要硬凑T0阵容，环境适配 > 角色强度。

🎯 F2P Full-Star Core Strategy: Use Pyro/Electro main DPS first half to break 12-2 Cryo shields, use Dendro/Hydro main DPS second half for Electro shields. Don't force T0 comps — matchup advantage > character strength.
