💧 角色定位 · 双形态机制详解Role & Dual-Arkhe Mechanics

芙宁娜 — 五星水元素单手剑角色，定位为增伤辅助 + 副C输出。核心机制是「荒/芒」双始基力形态切换：荒形态（黑芙）召唤三只海鲜自动攻击并消耗全队HP叠增伤；芒形态（白芙）召唤一个治疗歌者持续回血。

Furina — 5★ Hydro Sword character. Role: DMG buffer + Sub-DPS. Her core mechanic is the Ousia/Pneuma dual-Arkhe stance switch: Ousia (Dark Furina) summons three Salon Members that auto-attack while draining team HP to stack DMG bonus; Pneuma (Light Furina) summons a Singer of Many Waters for continuous healing.

一句话总结：芙宁娜是全游戏最通用的增伤辅助——给任何队伍带上她就能获得 50%~75% 的全队增伤（0命），同时她自身拥有不俗的副C输出（E技能自动脱手）。代价是持续烧血，需要带治疗角色搭配。

TL;DR: Furina is the most universal DMG buffer in the game — slot her into any team for 50%–75% team-wide DMG bonus (C0), while also dealing substantial off-field Sub-DPS (Skill auto-attacks). The trade-off is constant HP drain, so bring a healer.

📊 叠层机制

📊 Fanfare Stacking Mechanics

芙宁娜开启大招后进入「万众狂欢」状态，持续18秒。期间队友血量每波动1%（增或减），叠加1层「气氛值」。0命上限300层 = 75%全队增伤。C1初始就有150层，更快叠满。CD 15秒，可实现近乎全覆盖。

After casting her Burst, Furina enters the "Let the People Rejoice" state for 18s. Every 1% HP fluctuation (increase or decrease) from any party member grants 1 Fanfare stack. C0 cap: 300 stacks = 75% team DMG bonus. C1 starts at 150 stacks for faster ramp-up. 15s cooldown enables near 100% uptime.

🗡️ 武器选择 · 6档排名Weapon Ranking (6 Tiers)

芙宁娜的武器需求：暴伤/暴率 > 充能 > HP%。4万+血量是基础门槛，充能需求约180-200%（双水队可降至160%）。

Furina's weapon priority: CRIT DMG/CRIT Rate > ER > HP%. 40K+ HP is the baseline threshold; ER requirement is roughly 180–200% (drops to 160% in double-Hydro teams).

❓ 常见问题 FAQFAQ

生命沙还是充能沙？

HP Sands or ER Sands?

面板充能低于180%（单水队）时选充能沙。双水队或副词条充能足够时换生命沙，增伤收益更高。

Pick ER Sands if your ER is below 180% (solo-Hydro). Switch to HP Sands in double-Hydro teams or when substats provide enough ER for higher DMG returns.

剧团4还是千岩4？

Golden Troupe or Tenacity?

首选剧团4。千岩4仅用于纯辅助定位（如给赛诺队），损失自身输出不值得。

Golden Troupe 4pc is the pick. Tenacity 4pc is only for pure support roles (e.g. Cyno teams) — not worth sacrificing Furina's own damage output.

0命要抽吗？

Should I pull C0?

绝对要抽。0命芙宁娜已经是最通用的增伤辅助，能塞进90%的队伍。深渊/幽境危战/大世界通吃。

Absolutely yes. C0 Furina is already the most universal DMG buffer in the game, fitting into 90% of teams. Viable in Abyss, Imaginarium Theater, and open-world.
