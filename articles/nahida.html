🌿 角色定位 · 标记机制详解Role & Mark Mechanic

纳西妲 — 五星草元素法器角色，定位为副C + 草元素附着核心 + 全队增益。核心机制是元素战技「所闻遍计」标记敌人，当标记敌人触发任何元素反应时，对所有标记敌人同时造成草元素伤害（Tri-Karma Purification）。元素爆发「心景幻梦」展开巨大领域，根据队伍元素组成强化队友。

Nahida — 5★ Dendro Catalyst character. Role: Sub-DPS + Dendro applier + team buffer. Core mechanic: Elemental Skill "All Schemes to Know" marks enemies; when ANY marked enemy triggers an elemental reaction, ALL marked enemies take Tri-Karma Purification Dendro DMG simultaneously. Elemental Burst "Illusory Heart" creates a massive field that buffs the team based on party element composition.

一句话总结：纳西妲是所有草反应队的无可争议女王——她的E技能提供全游戏最优秀的脱手草附着，Q技能根据队伍元素提供团队增益。任何草反应队有她都质变。

TL;DR: Nahida is the undisputed queen of all Dendro reaction teams — her E provides the best off-field Dendro application in the game, and her Q buffs the team based on party elements. Any Dendro team improves drastically with her.

🔑 核心天赋优先级

🔑 Talent Priority

元素战技 (E) > 元素爆发 (Q) >> 普通攻击 (A)Elemental Skill (E) > Elemental Burst (Q) >> Normal Attack (A)

E是核心脱手输出+草附着手段，Q提供团队增益，普攻完全不点（C6以前无用）。

Skill is the core off-field DPS + Dendro application. Burst provides team buffs. Normal Attack can be ignored entirely (useless before C6).

📊 标记机制详解

📊 Mark Mechanic Details

E技能「所闻遍计」标记最多8个敌人。当任意标记敌人触发反应（绽放/超绽放/烈绽放/激化/燃烧/扩散/激化/超激化）时，所有标记敌人同时受到Tri-Karma Purification草元素伤害。这是全游戏最优秀的草元素附着机制。

Skill "All Schemes to Know" marks up to 8 enemies. When ANY marked enemy triggers a reaction (Bloom/Hyperbloom/Burgeon/Quicken/Burning/Spread/Aggravate), ALL marked enemies receive Tri-Karma Purification Dendro DMG simultaneously. This is the best Dendro application mechanic in the game.

🧠 被动天赋（A4）

🧠 Passive Talent (A4)

纳西妲的元素精通超过200的部分，每点额外元素精通提供：E技能0.1%暴击率（上限24%）和0.03%伤害加成（上限80%）。1000元素精通封顶。这意味着堆元素精通同时提升暴击率和伤害。

Nahida's EM above 200 grants up to 24% CRIT Rate and 80% DMG bonus to her E (0.1% CRIT Rate and 0.03% DMG per EM above 200, capped at 1000 EM). This means building EM simultaneously boosts both CRIT Rate and DMG.

🗡️ 武器选择 · 7档排名Weapon Ranking (7 Tiers)

纳西妲的武器需求：元素精通 > 暴击/暴伤 > 攻击力%。她是全游戏极少数元素精通直接转化为输出的角色，优先选择高元素精通武器。

Nahida's weapon priority: EM > CRIT/CRIT DMG > ATK%. She is one of the very few characters who directly convert EM into output. Prioritize high EM weapons.

💎 圣遗物搭配 · 主词条与面板目标Artifacts & Stat Targets

首选套装：深林的记忆 ×4（深林4）

Best Set: Deepwood Memories ×4

2件套：草元素伤害加成 +15%
4件套：元素战技或元素爆发命中敌人后，使命中敌人的草元素抗性降低30%，持续8秒（可脱手触发）。

2pc: Dendro DMG Bonus +15%
4pc: After Elemental Skill or Burst hits an enemy, enemy Dendro RES -30% for 8s (works off-field).

深林4是草反应队指定辅助套装——纳西妲必须携带，除非队伍中已有其他角色装备深林4。脱手触发，覆盖率接近100%。

Deepwood 4 is THE support set for Dendro reaction teams — Nahida must carry it unless someone else on the team already has Deepwood 4. Triggers off-field with near 100% uptime.

🔄 深林4 vs 饰金4

🔄 Deepwood 4 vs Gilded 4

绝大多数情况穿深林4。只有确定队伍中已有其他角色（如白术、瑶瑶）装备深林4时，纳西妲才切换为饰金4或2+2 EM散件，最大化自身输出。

Wear Deepwood 4 in almost all cases. Only switch to Gilded 4 or 2pc+2pc EM if another teammate (e.g., Baizhu, Yaoyao) is already using Deepwood 4, to maximize her own output.

❓ 常见问题 FAQFAQ

头冠选元素精通还是暴击？

EM or CRIT circlet?

BUFF后元素精通低于850→选元素精通头。850以上→选暴击率/暴伤头。1000元素精通是A4被动的硬上限，超过后不再获得暴击率和伤害加成。

Below 850 EM after buffs → pick EM circlet. Above 850 EM → CRIT Rate/DMG circlet. 1000 EM is the hard cap for her A4 passive — beyond that you gain no extra CRIT Rate or DMG bonus.

深林4还是饰金4？

Deepwood or Gilded?

纳西妲应在大多数队伍中穿深林4。只有确定队伍中已有其他角色（如白术、瑶瑶）装备深林4时，才切换为饰金4或2+2 EM散件。

Nahida should wear Deepwood 4 in most teams. Only switch to Gilded 4 or 2pc+2pc EM if another teammate (e.g., Baizhu, Yaoyao) is already using Deepwood 4.

0命值得抽吗？

Is C0 worth pulling?

必抽。纳西妲是所有草反应队中单角色影响力最大的单位。任何草反应队有她都会产生质变提升。0命即毕业级别。

Must-pull. Nahida is the single most impactful Dendro unit. Any Dendro team improves drastically with her. C0 is already graduation-level.
