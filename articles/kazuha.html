🍁 角色定位 · A4扩散增伤机制详解Role & A4 Swirl DMG Buff Mechanics

枫原万叶 — 五星风元素单手剑角色，定位为元素增伤辅助 + 聚怪 + 副C输出。核心机制是固有天赋「风物之诗咏」：万叶触发扩散反应后，每1点元素精通为全队提供0.04%对应元素伤害加成。1000精通的万叶可提供40%元素伤害加成，配合翠绿之影4件套再削减敌人40%元素抗性，一个角色实现增伤+减抗双覆盖。

Kaedehara Kazuha — 5★ Anemo Sword character. Role: Elemental DMG Buffer + Crowd Control + Sub-DPS. His core mechanic is the A4 passive "Poetics of Fuubutsu": after triggering a Swirl, Kazuha grants 0.04% Elemental DMG bonus per point of EM to the entire party. At 1000 EM, that's 40% Elemental DMG bonus. Combined with 4pc Viridescent Venerer shredding 40% enemy RES, one character provides both DMG buffing and RES shredding.

一句话总结：万叶是公认的原神最强风系增伤辅助——E技能提供聚怪和强力的下落攻击，Q技能展开大范围风元素领域并吸收元素进行持续扩散。配合A4天赋和风套4，一个角色实现增伤、减抗、聚怪三合一。

TL;DR: Kazuha is the undisputed best Anemo support in Genshin Impact — his E skill provides grouping (CC) with a powerful plunge attack, his Q creates a large Anemo field with elemental absorption. Combined with his A4 passive and VV 4pc, one character handles DMG buffing, RES shredding, and crowd control simultaneously.

📊 A4扩散增伤机制

📊 A4 Swirl DMG Buff Mechanics

万叶触发扩散反应 → A4被动将精通转化为全队元素伤害加成（每种扩散元素独立计算）。1000精通 = 40%元素增伤。配合翠绿之影4件套进一步削减敌人元素抗性40%，持续10秒。通过合理操作可同时扩散多个元素，实现双增伤。

Kazuha triggers Swirl → A4 passive converts EM to team-wide Elemental DMG bonus (per swirled element). 1000 EM = 40% Elemental DMG bonus. Viridescent Venerer 4pc further shreds enemy Elemental RES by 40% for 10s. Multiple elements can be buffed simultaneously with proper rotation.

🗡️ 武器选择 · 6档排名Weapon Ranking (6 Tiers)

万叶的武器需求：元素精通 > 元素充能效率。精通是万叶的核心属性，直接决定A4被动提供的增伤数值。充能需求约为160-180%（单风队），有西风/祭礼或双风可降至140%。

Kazuha's weapon priority: Elemental Mastery > Energy Recharge. EM is Kazuha's core stat, directly determining the DMG bonus from his A4 passive. ER requirement is roughly 160–180% (solo Anemo), dropping to 140%+ with Favonius/Sacrificial or double-Anemo teams.

❓ 常见问题 FAQFAQ

圣遗物堆精通还是攻风暴？

EM or ATK/CRIT for build?

全堆精通（三精通主词条）是辅助万叶的唯一正确选择。攻/风/暴只适用于C6站场主C玩法。精通直接转化为全队增伤——越多越好，1000精通即为毕业。

Full EM (triple EM main stats) is the only correct build for support Kazuha. ATK/Anemo/CRIT is only for C6 on-field DPS. EM directly converts to team DMG bonus — the more the better, with 1000 EM as the graduation benchmark.

铁蜂刺还是西风剑？

Iron Sting or Favonius?

充能≥160%选铁蜂刺。充能不够选西风剑——全队充能收益远大于165精通。万叶开不出大招意味着丢失持续扩散和增伤覆盖，优先保证循环。

Iron Sting if ER ≥ 160%. Favonius if ER is struggling — the team energy is worth more than 165 EM. If Kazuha can't burst on cooldown, you lose continuous Swirl and DMG buff uptime — prioritize smooth rotations.

0命够用吗？

Is C0 enough?

完全够用。0命万叶已经是游戏最强增伤辅助——增伤+减抗+聚怪三合一的角色。C2是全队+200精通的豪华升级，C6是主C形态。先抽0命，再考虑武器，最后追命座。

Absolutely. C0 Kazuha is already the best support in the game — DMG buffing + RES shredding + crowd control all in one. C2 is a luxury team upgrade (+200 EM), C6 is for DPS Kazuha. Pull C0 first, then weapons, then constellations.
